Our Mission

We dream of a world where butterflies flourish within their native ecosystems, enriching biodiversity and ensuring the vitality of our natural landscapes across the United States. Our vision is for a future where the activities of humans seamlessly integrate with the natural world, nurturing the existence of these essential pollinators. Our mission is to enlighten and advocate for butterfly conservation, offering in-depth resources and captivating materials that underscore the critical ecological roles butterflies play, from pollination to signaling the health of our environment. Our goal is to motivate both individuals and communities to engage in significant efforts that bolster the populations of these indispensable insects.
